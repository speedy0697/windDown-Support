# Wind Down: Sleep Ritual — Support & Privacy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Wind Down ("Wind Down," "we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile app designed to help people build a short nightly wind-down ritual before sleep. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have.

If you use Wind Down, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Wind Down:

### A. Information you enter in the app

Depending on how you use Wind Down, you may provide:

- Brain dump entries
- Reflection responses
- Custom reflection prompts you create
- Bedtime struggle selections and personalization settings
- Wind-down time, wake time, and notification preferences
- Sleep rating check-ins

This information is primarily stored locally on your device.

### B. Apple Sign In information

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we may store the following information provided by Apple:

- Your Apple user identifier
- Your email address, if Apple shares it
- Your name, if Apple shares it

Using Sign in with Apple is optional.

### C. Subscription and purchase information

If you purchase a subscription or restore purchases, Wind Down uses RevenueCat to help manage subscriptions and entitlements. We do not directly process or store your full payment card information.

Apple handles billing for App Store purchases.

### D. Analytics and app usage information

Wind Down uses PostHog to collect product analytics, such as:

- App opens
- Onboarding progress
- Ritual starts and completions
- Feature usage
- Notification interactions
- Purchase and restore events
- High-level subscription and streak status

These analytics help us understand how the app is used, improve reliability, and decide what to build next.

### E. Device and technical information

Our analytics and service providers may receive limited technical information such as:

- Device or app instance identifiers
- Platform and app version information
- Basic diagnostic and event metadata

### F. Notifications

If you allow notifications, Wind Down schedules local notifications on your device, including:

- Nightly ritual reminders
- Rescue reminders
- Morning recall reminders based on your prior brain dump entries

The content of those reminders may include text you entered in the app, such as the items you wanted to remember the next morning.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide the Wind Down experience
- Save your ritual history and preferences
- Personalize prompts, ritual flow, and reminders
- Schedule and deliver local notifications
- Enable optional Sign in with Apple
- Manage subscriptions and premium access
- Measure usage and improve the app
- Diagnose bugs, performance issues, and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How Information Is Stored

Much of the personal journal-style content you enter into Wind Down, including brain dump items, reflections, settings, and ritual history, is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

Some information is also shared with service providers that help us operate the app, including analytics and subscription infrastructure.

At this time, Wind Down is not designed as a cloud journaling service and does not promise permanent backup or cross-device syncing of all in-app entries.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information:

- With service providers that help us operate Wind Down
- If required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or the public
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or restructuring

## 6. Data Retention

Because much of your content is stored locally on your device, retention may depend on:

- Whether you keep the app installed
- Whether you clear app data
- Whether you delete the app
- Whether you remove content yourself

Information processed by third-party providers may be retained according to their own retention practices and our service configuration.

## 7. Your Choices

You can choose to:

- Not use Sign in with Apple
- Disable notifications in the app or in your device settings
- Remove the app from your device
- Delete local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing device/app storage where available
- Avoid purchasing a subscription

If you signed in with Apple, you can also stop using that feature from within the app.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Wind Down is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will review the request.

## 9. International Users

If you use Wind Down outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. Those countries may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

## 10. Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information handled through Wind Down. However, no method of storage, transmission, or processing is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Because some content is stored locally on your device, the security of that information also depends on your device security, operating system protections, and whether other people can access your device.

## 11. Mental Health and Sensitive Content

Wind Down may contain journal-style or emotionally sensitive content that you choose to enter. Wind Down is a wellness product and is not a medical, psychiatric, or crisis service.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent mental health support, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis resource.
